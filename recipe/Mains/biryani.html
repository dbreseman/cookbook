Biryani

.cook

Layered, aromatic rice and meat dish.

👥 8 servings 📖 https://flavoredbyfatima.com/2020/02/19/memoni-deghi-biryani/

🥘 Ingredients

Fried Shallots & Potatoes

  • fried-shallots
    1 batch
  • potatoes (Yukon Gold)
    3

Gravy

  • Bombay Biryani Masala (Shan brand)
    3 tbsp
  • bay leaf
    1
  • black cardamom
    1
  • cauliflower (florets)
    300 g
  • cinnamon stick
    1
  • dried cranberries
    4 tbsp
  • ginger-garlic paste
    2 tbsp
  • green beans (chopped)
    200 g
  • lemon juice
    1/2 lemon
  • meat (goat, lamb, or chicken thighs, skinless and bone-in)
    800 g
  • red onions (finely diced)
    300 g
  • salt (10g)
    2 tsp
  • tomatoes (finely diced)
    300 g
  • turmeric (2g)
    1 tsp
  • water (240ml)
    1 c
  • yogurt (120g)
    1/2 c

Rice

  • basmati rice (500g)
    2.5 c
  • bay leaf
    1
  • black cardamom
    1 pod
  • cinnamon stick
    1
  • cumin seeds
    1 tbsp
  • green cardamom
    4 pods
  • salt
    3 tbsp
  • water
    8 c
  • white vinegar
    3 tbsp

Saffron Cream & Herbs

  • cilantro
    4 tbsp
  • cream (60ml)
    4 tbsp
  • mint
    4 tbsp
  • saffron
    1 pinch

Fried Shallots & Potatoes

  1. 1
    Make 1 batch fried-shallots .
    fried-shallots: 1 batch
  2. 2
    Peel and quarter potatoes . Return reserved shallot oil to pot, place over high heat, and cook potatoes until golden brown. Remove potatoes and set oil aside.
    potatoes: 3 (Yukon Gold)

Gravy

  1. 1
    In a large bowl, combine meat with yogurt , lemon juice , turmeric , Bombay Biryani Masala , and salt . Let marinate for at least ⏱️ 30 minutes .
    meat: 800 g (goat, lamb, or chicken thighs, skinless and bone-in), yogurt: 1/2 c (120g), lemon juice: 1/2 lemon, turmeric: 1 tsp (2g), Bombay Biryani Masala: 3 tbsp (Shan brand), salt: 2 tsp (10g)
  2. 2
    In a dutch oven, add 1/4 cup of the reserved shallot oil and saute red onions until translucent. Add ginger-garlic paste , cinnamon stick , black cardamom , bay leaf , and tomatoes . Cook on medium-low until the oil starts to separate.
    red onions: 300 g (finely diced), ginger-garlic paste: 2 tbsp, cinnamon stick: 1, black cardamom: 1, bay leaf: 1, tomatoes: 300 g (finely diced)
  3. 3
    Add meat and marinade and cook until tender—about ⏱️ 30 minutes for chicken and ⏱️ 2 hours for goat or lamb. If using goat or lamb, add water at this step. Taste and adjust flavoring as needed.
    water: 1 c (240ml)
  4. 4
    Add fried potatoes, cauliflower , green beans , and dried cranberries for the last ⏱️ 10 minutes .
    cauliflower: 300 g (florets), green beans: 200 g (chopped), dried cranberries: 4 tbsp

Rice

  1. 1
    Rinse basmati rice until clear, then soak for 10 to ⏱️ 20 minutes . Meanwhile, boil water in a separate pot along with bay leaf , black cardamom , green cardamom , cinnamon stick , and cumin seeds .
    basmati rice: 2.5 c (500g), water: 8 c, bay leaf: 1, black cardamom: 1 pod, green cardamom: 4 pods, cinnamon stick: 1, cumin seeds: 1 tbsp
  2. 2
    Once vigorously boiling, add white vinegar , salt , and rice. Cook until the pot comes back to a boil and the rice is about 70% done, ⏱️ 10 minutes (the grains should be softened on the outside but still have a firm, hard core when bitten into). Drain and rinse in cold water. Set aside.
    white vinegar: 3 tbsp, salt: 3 tbsp

Saffron Cream & Herbs

  1. 1
    Grind saffron in a mortar and pestle to a powder, then add to a bowl with cream . Microwave until well infused.
    saffron: 1 pinch, cream: 4 tbsp (60ml)
  2. 2
    Chiffonade cilantro and mint .
    cilantro: 4 tbsp, mint: 4 tbsp

Layering & Baking

  1. 1
    Preheat oven to 170°C.
  2. 2
    Now layer the biryani: spread half of the saffron cream on the bottom of the dutch oven, then top with a thin layer of rice. Then add the meat and vegetables, then the rice, herbs, and half the fried shallots in a couple layers. Thin out remaining saffron cream with some water and sprinkle on top of the rice.
  3. 3
    Set it on high for the first 5 to ⏱️ 10 minutes uncovered, then cover and transfer to preheated oven. Let bake for ⏱️ 20 minutes , then remove and let sit for ⏱️ 10 minutes .

Serving

  1. 1
    Serve with cucumber-pomegranate raita and remaining fried shallots.
