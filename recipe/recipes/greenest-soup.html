The Greenest Broccoli Soup

.cook
👥 5 servings 🍽️ Soup 📖 https://www.seriouseats.com/broccoli-soup-recipe-8772702

🥘 Ingredients

Blanch and Puree

  • baking soda (6g)
    1 tsp
  • broccoli (450g, roughly chopped)
    4 c
  • leek (170g, washed and chopped, divided)
    1.5 c
  • parsley (30g, chopped)
    2 c
  • salt (12g, divided)
    4 tsp
  • snap peas (115g, trimmed)
    1 c
  • water

Build Soup Base

  • black pepper (3g)
    1 tsp
  • chicken bouillon paste
    1 tsp
  • fish sauce (15ml)
    1 tbsp
  • garlic cloves (10g, grated)
    2 cloves
  • leek
  • salt
  • vegetable oil

Puree

  • cashews
    15
  • water

Assemble and Serve

  • black pepper
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • lemon
  • salt

Blanch and Puree

  1. 1
    In a large pot, boil 12 cups of water , 1 tablespoon of the salt , and baking soda until evenly distributed. Bring the liquid to a boil over high heat.
    water, salt: 4 tsp (12g, divided), baking soda: 1 tsp (6g)
  2. 2
    Blanch broccoli and snap peas until vibrant green and tender, ⏱️ 5 minutes to ⏱️ 6 minutes . Use a slotted spoon to remove the greens and shock in ice water.
    broccoli: 4 c (450g, roughly chopped), snap peas: 1 c (115g, trimmed)
  3. 3
    Return the water to a boil, then add the sliced dark green tops of the leek and the parsley . Cook until vibrant green and wilted, ⏱️ 1 minute to ⏱️ 2 minutes longer. Drain the vegetables and discard the cooking water.
    leek: 1.5 c (170g, washed and chopped, divided), parsley: 2 c (30g, chopped)

Build Soup Base

  1. 1
    In the empty Dutch oven, heat the remaining 3 tablespoons of vegetable oil and black pepper over medium-low heat until the pepper begins to sizzle, about ⏱️ 30 seconds .
    vegetable oil, black pepper: 1 tsp (3g)
  2. 2
    Add the white and light green chopped parts of the leek and the remaining 1 teaspoon of salt . Cook, stirring occasionally, until the vegetables soften, about ⏱️ 3 minutes .
    leek, salt
  3. 3
    Stir in garlic cloves and chicken bouillon paste and cook until fragrant, about ⏱️ 30 seconds .
    garlic cloves: 2 cloves (10g, grated), chicken bouillon paste: 1 tsp
  4. 4
    Stir in fish sauce and cook until slightly evaporated, about ⏱️ 15 seconds .
    fish sauce: 1 tbsp (15ml)

Puree

  1. 1
    Transfer the soup base, blanched vegetables, and cashews to a blender. Add 2 cups of cold water and blend on high speed until mostly smooth, ⏱️ 30 seconds to ⏱️ 60 seconds . Return the puree to the heat and heat through, about ⏱️ 1 minute .
    cashews: 15, water

Assemble and Serve

  1. 1
    Remove the soup from the heat. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
    salt, black pepper
  2. 2
    Ladle the soup into individual bowls. Drizzle with extra-virgin olive oil and a squeeze of lemon .
    extra-virgin olive oil, lemon
